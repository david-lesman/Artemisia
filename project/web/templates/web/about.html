What we're doing

Artemisia is an interactive and responsive web app built using multiple technologies.

Utilising fun and child-friendly graphics and minigames to educate children on Diabetes mellitus, we want to decrease the lack of knowledge and make kids act responsibly with and around those with this disease.

Why we're doing this

While searching for project ideas, we decided to reflect on our day-to-day problems. One of our team members, Youri, brought up that when he was diagnosed with type 1 Diabetes mellitus at the age of 8, it was difficult for him and the kids around him to understand what it meant.

This problem inspired us to come up with our project Artemisia.
